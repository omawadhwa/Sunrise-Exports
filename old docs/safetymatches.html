Safety Matches

We are leading manufacturers of safety matchbox established in the year 2000 & our matchboxes are widely exported & accepted all over Africa, Middle East & Asia. We offer a wide assortment of safety matchbox & can manufacture to any size, brand and packing style as desired by our customer. Our matchbox are of high grade and they do not spark or flame at the time of burning. Usage of good quality wood in manufacturing splints makes these matches efficient in performance & safe to use. Our products are most suitable for humid climate and are adequately treated to withstand high humidity to withstand high humidity without compromising on performance. These matchboxes are made from superior quality raw materials to provide extra glow in a strike. Available in different quantities of packs, these matchboxes are highly safe for all kind of lighting purposes. Matchboxes are available in regular size with wooden sticks,kitchen matchbox (Big size) and wax matchbox with sticks made from wax dipped paper.
